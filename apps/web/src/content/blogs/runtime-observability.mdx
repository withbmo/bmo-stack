# Runtime observability in production

Observability should not be an afterthought. Teams move faster when logs and traces are available from the first deploy.

## Why this matters

You can detect regressions quickly, reduce incident resolution time, and understand usage patterns with less guesswork.

## Practical checklist

- Enable structured logs for every service.
- Track latency and error rate for critical endpoints.
- Add deploy annotations to your dashboards.
- Define alert thresholds for high-priority APIs.

## Final note

Start with a small baseline and iterate. Consistency is more important than complexity at the beginning.
